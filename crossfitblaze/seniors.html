Common Questions from Seniors

"Will I get injured?"

Safety is our #1 priority. We focus on proper form, gradual progression, and workouts tailored to your abilities. Our coaches are experienced in working with older adults and common age-related concerns.

"Do I need to be in shape first?"

Not at all! You don't get in shape to do CrossFit ‚Äî you do CrossFit to get in shape. We meet you exactly where you are.

"What if I have arthritis/joint issues?"

Many of our senior members have arthritis or past injuries. We modify movements to reduce impact while still building strength and mobility. Always consult your doctor, but we've helped many seniors with similar concerns.

"Will I be the only older person there?"

We have a growing community of members 50+, including several in their 60s and 70s. We also offer classes specifically geared toward older adults.
